Gatekeeper Sija

Sija is an expert Zearer Mage and also is a very large female. She's been prohibited from learning magic as a child but later escaped her home to learn more. During this she got a place as a gatekeeper for the wall that split the Julla Lands from the Amish Lands (Random names btw). When she got to adulthood she casted a very powerful spell on herself which transformed her into a very busty girl that can't get pregnant.
